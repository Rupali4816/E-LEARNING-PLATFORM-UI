Photography

Photography is the art and science of capturing images using a camera, with the goal of preserving memories, telling stories, and conveying emotions. It involves a combination of technical skills, such as understanding lighting, composition, and focus, as well as creative vision and attention to detail. Photographers use various techniques, including portrait, landscape, still life, and documentary photography, to capture a wide range of subjects and scenes. With the rise of digital technology, photography has become more accessible and widespread, allowing people to express themselves and share their perspectives with others.
